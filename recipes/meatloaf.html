Mom's Legenday Loaf


Description

my moms easy to make meatloaf

Thing you will need:

  • 1lbs ground beef
  • 1lg onion
  • green bellpepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 sleeve ritz crackers
  • 1 egg
  • 1tbs yellow mustard
  • 1tsb hard to say sauce
  1. pre heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. finely chop onion, bellpepper,and garlic
  3. add ground beef to bowl
  4. crush sleeve of ritz crackers and add to bowl
  5. add in veggies, mustard, egg, and hard to spell sauce
  6. roll into loaf
  7. place on nonstick pan
  8. cook for 1 hour
